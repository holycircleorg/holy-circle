About Holy Circle

Rooted in Scripture • Driven by Purpose

Welcome to Holy Circle

A message from our team about the heart, mission, and vision behind Holy Circle.

Mission Statement

Holy Circle exists to inspire and empower individuals and families to deepen their faith in Christ through engaging content and community connections, encouraging active participation in building God’s kingdom on Earth.

“And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds.” — Hebrews 10:24–25

Statement of Faith

1. The Written Word of God

The Bible (66 books) is the inspired, authoritative Word of God.

2. God

One God in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

3. Salvation

By grace through faith in Jesus Christ — received through repentance and belief.

4. Eternity

Eternal life with Christ for believers; separation from God for those who reject Him.

5. The World

God created humanity in His image; redemption is found in Christ alone.

What We Do

Holy Circle is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit using media, events, and entertainment to help people encounter Jesus in their everyday lives.

  • Podcast & Conversations — real stories and biblical truth for modern life.
  • Films & Visual Storytelling — creative projects that point people to Christ.
  • Community & Events — Worship, outreach, and discipleship moments.

Our Story

Where it all began — and where God is taking us.

Holy Circle began as a spark — a desire to create a place where people could encounter Jesus through creativity, storytelling, and genuine community.

What started as conversations around faith has grown into a nonprofit focused on building disciples through media, worship gatherings, outreach, and cinematic storytelling.

Our heart is simple: to reach people where they are, and point them toward Jesus.
